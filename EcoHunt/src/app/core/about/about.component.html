About Us

Welcome to EcoHunt! We are dedicated to promoting environmental conservation through community engagement and innovative solutions. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to take action in preserving the natural beauty of our planet.

At EcoHunt, we believe that small steps can lead to big changes. Our platform allows users to create and participate in environmental clean-up events, track and report pollution, and collaborate with like-minded individuals and organizations.

Join us in our journey to make the world a cleaner and greener place. Together, we can make a difference!

Our Goals

  • Reduce pollution through organized clean-up events
  • Educate the public on environmental issues
  • Foster a sense of community and responsibility towards the environment
